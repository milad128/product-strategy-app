# Metrics Glossary — BNPL (Unsecured)

## Core metrics

| Metric | Symbol | Definition |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Gross Merchandise Value | GMV | Total transaction value paid via BNPL (unsecured) credit in a given period |
| Average Order Value | AOV | Average monetary value per transaction: `GMV / # Transactions` |
| Orders Per Buyer | OPB | Average orders per unique buyer: `# Transactions / # Buyers` |
| Transactions | # Transactions | Count of completed BNPL (unsecured) purchase transactions |
| Buyers | # Buyers | Count of unique customers who completed at least one transaction |

## AOV components

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| Credit Spent | Portion of AOV paid using allocated BNPL credit |
| Debit Spent | Portion of AOV paid using non-credit payment (debit/cash) |
| Spend Rate | Share of allocated credit that is actually spent per order or period |
| Allocated Credit | Credit limit or line assigned to the customer |

## Marketplace expansion (growth equation)

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| Sellers Listing Items | Count of sellers actively listing items eligible for BNPL (unsecured) |
| Listed Items | Count of items listed and available for BNPL purchase |

## Buyer-side rates

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| Purchase Rate | Share of customers in a segment who complete a purchase in the period |
| Activation Rate | Share of fresh credit holders who complete their first purchase |
| Allocation Rate | Share of applicants who receive a credit allocation |
| Second Chance Rate | Share of rejected applicants who convert via a second-chance flow |
| Holder Revenant Rate | Share of dead credit holders who return and transact again |
