# Credit Product Lines

## Overview

This app tracks product strategy across multiple credit products. Version 1 focuses on a single product line.

| Product | Code | Status in v1 |
|---------|------|--------------|
| BNPL (Unsecured) | `bnpl-unsecure` | **Active** — full definitions and strategy |
| BNPL (Secured) | `bnpl-secure` | Planned — not yet defined |
| C-Credit | `c-credit` | Planned — not yet defined |

## BNPL (Unsecured)

Buy Now, Pay Later without collateral. Customers receive a credit line to purchase from marketplace sellers. Strategy is driven by the **Growth Equation** with **GMV** as the north star metric.

All metric definitions, input drivers, and buyer segments for v1 refer to this product line unless stated otherwise.

_To be defined._ Expected to share structural similarities with BNPL (Unsecured) but with secured/collateral-specific drivers.
